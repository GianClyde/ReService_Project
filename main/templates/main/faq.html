Frequently asked questions?

Through the "Prepared Environment," IMC has consistently maintained a great academic program, providing the best quality education necessary for the "whole man concept" of positively developing pupils academically, emotionally, mentally, socially, psychologically, and spiritually.

IMC assists children in developing the foundations for creative learning attitudes and abilities that are necessary for academic and life success. It provides a thirst for learning, concentration, initiative, order, persistence, creativity and originality.

Teachers are carefully chosen not only for their professional skills, but also for their understanding of and affection for children. Teachers gain fulfillment from working to make the world a better place for tomorrow's adults.
